Verizon

UX Design / Front-End Development

My Internship

I designed and developed a new mobile application for Verizon Partner Solutions within 10 weeks. On a team of 2 other interns, I served as the UX specialist, leading the UX research and design process.

After completing the final digital prototype, I contributed to front-end development and testing. This project strengthened my research, visual design, and front-end development skills.

Timeline: Summer 2019, 10 weeks

My Role: UX Design & Development Intern

My Department: Verizon Partner Solutions

My Takeaways

Throughout my internship, I gained confidence in my background in UX. My success leading real-world research and design sessions while keeping up with 2 software engineers on front-end development showed me the value in my unique design and coding background.

I also learned the importance of incorporating updated technology into every industry. While other areas of Verizon launched huge endeavors like 5G, the telecommunications wholesale industry took a huge step towards improving their customer experience by creating a mobile application.

My summer at Verizon gave me inspiration to continue pursuing a career where I can help my company pursue the next big thing by creating solutions to help users in their everyday lives.
