About Me

I'm a junior at the University of Michigan, pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information and a Minor in Business. I'm passionate about understanding users and their requirements in order to create innovative interfaces that solve real problems.

My natural creativity paired with my analytical mindset has led me to pursue a career in User Experience Research and Design. Not only do I enjoy taking UX courses, such as User Interaction Design Studio and Graphic Design, but I also love expanding my front-end development and data manipulation skills.

Why don't I just focus on UX? I love gaining experience across different areas of tech to have a wholistic understanding of what it takes to develop a product from start to finish. I'm proud of my design, coding, and interpersonal skills and believe that this combination will help me succeed in a career in UX.

When I'm not in my favorite spot in the Law Library, you'll find me around campus singing in my acappella group or designing apparel for my professional business fraternity. There's nothing I love talking about more than Michigan, so I'm also fortunate to work as a campus tour guide in my free time.

I'd love to talk about anything from UX to Michigan Football, so let's chat! Thanks for stopping by and go blue.
